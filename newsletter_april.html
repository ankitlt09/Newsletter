PLAYER OF THE MONTH

 
 
    

        Mohd Danish aka Eyerise has been declared as IGL's player of the
        Month for March 2020.Danish has remained undefeated in 8 IGL
        competitions and has ranked amongst the top 3 in 24 competitions
        on the IGL platform in March.
        Eyerise has participated in a number of tournaments including
        Pubg Mobile,Call Of Duty Mobile,8 Ball Pool,Ludo King,Smashing Four
        and more.He has proven to be invincible on multiple occassions,
        achieving podium finishes unlike any other player on IGL.
        Eyerise has won over 8000 IGL coins in the month of March itself.         #ItPaysToPlay
 
 

 
 

IGL NEWS

 
    
  On 29 February 2020, IGL will host its first Pokémon Sword/ Shield
  tournament.The gaming platform for the tournament will be Nintendo
  Switch.The tournament will kick-off at 4.00 pm and the check-in for
  the same will start at 3.00 pm, one hour prior to the tournament and
  will end at 3.55 pm.
  The prize pool for the tournament will be 1000 IGL Coins.The winner
  of the tournament will be rewarded 700 IGL Coins while the runner-up
  will win 300 IGL Coins.You need to have Nintendo Live to participate
  in this tournament.
   #ItPaysToPlay
 
 
     
  IGL will introduce Smashing four and Asphalt 9: Legends in the first

  Thursday, 5 March 2020. The tournament will start at 6.00 pm and the

  at 5.00 pm.The prize pool for both the tournaments will be 1000 IGL
